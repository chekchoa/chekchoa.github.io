Court Schedule & Access

Normal Operating Hours

10:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Open to all registered CHE/HOA members and their accompanied guests.
  • Courts are strictly reserved for recreational tennis and pickleball play.
  • Children and guests should be informed of facility policies by the responsible community member to ensure a safe environment for everyone.

Evening Closing Policy

To ensure the security of our community facility, please plan to exit the courts before our scheduled 9:00 PM closing time.

Our automated neighborhood access keys deactivate promptly at closing. Gathering your equipment and leaving a few minutes early helps prevent an accidental lock-in within the facility gates.

Please note that remaining inside after hours may lead to a temporary suspension of community facility access privileges, and off-hours gate assistance can involve significant response delays.

Court Rules & Guidelines

Surface Preservation: The tennis and pickleball courts are a major neighborhood asset. Keeping improper footwear, wheeled equipment, and staining materials off the courts preserves the specialized playing surface and keeps maintenance costs low.

Surface Prohibitions

  • No Pets Allowed: Animals are strictly prohibited inside the fenced court area.
  • No Wheeled Equipment: Bicycles, scooters, roller skates, inline skates, and rollerblades are not permitted.
  • No Toys or Motorized Vehicles: Battery-powered child vehicles are strictly banned from the surface.
  • No Sidewalk Chalk: Chalk damages the specialized grip coating and is not allowed.

Footwear & Proper Use

  • Proper Footwear Required: Please wear white, soft-soled athletic shoes only.
  • No Hard-Soled Shoes: Dark, heavy, or hard-soled shoes scratch, mar, and scuff the surface.
  • Active Players Only: If you are not actively playing tennis or pickleball, you should not be on the courts.
  • Guest & Child Supervision: Members are responsible for ensuring that their children and guests understand and follow these rules.

⚠️ Important Safety Notice

Play at Your Own Risk: All residents and guests use the courts strictly at their own risk. The HOA assumes no liability for injuries sustained on the premises.

Video Surveillance: For the safety and security of our neighborhood, the tennis and pickleball facility may be under 24/7 video surveillance.

Unsafe Conditions: Unsafe conditions must be reported immediatelly, for everyone's safety the courts should be vacated until all unsafe conditions are addressed

🔑 Electronic Access Key Tags

Carriage Hill Estates utilizes an electronic key tag system to ensure secure, private access to our sports facilities for members in good standing.

  • Account Status: Household HOA dues and outstanding balances must be fully paid to maintain access. Key tags are automatically restricted for accounts not in good standing.
  • Replacement Fee: If your key tag is lost, misplaced, or requires a replacement, a $25 fee applies.
  • Household Limit: There is a strict limit of one active key tag per household, regardless of the number of residents.
  • Resolution Time: Contact the board to restore access or order a replacement. The process typically takes a few business days to complete.
