Welcome to Our Neighborhood

Whether you are a prospective homebuyer exploring the area or a new neighbor settling in, Carriage Hill Estates offers the perfect blend of quiet suburban charm, exceptional shared amenities, and fast accessibility to everything Kansas City has to offer.

Maintained proudly by an active, community-focused volunteer Homeowners Association, our neighborhood prioritizes safety, visual appeal, friendly interactions, and long-term property values. We invite you to explore what makes our community a wonderful place to call home.

Community Amenities

🏊 Private Community Pool

Our private neighborhood pool serves as the community's central gathering point during warm summer months. Outfitted with excellent shade fixtures and dynamic early workout schedules, it is ideal for neighborhood fun or family relaxation.

  • Dedicated adult exercise & lap swimming hours.
  • Shaded pergola and rentable party spaces.
  • Secure, keyless electronic gate access system.

🎾 Tennis & Pickleball Courts

Stay active just a short stroll from your front door. Our newly updated and meticulously maintained court facilities feature high-performance sports surfaces perfect for standard tennis matches or the growing trend of casual pickleball games.

  • Private fenced facility reserved strictly for residents.
  • Optimized and grip-coated specialized playing surface.
  • Open daily with family-friendly recreation hours.

🏡 Well-Maintained Environs

Our volunteer board and dedicated architectural review processes ensure that Carriage Hill Estates consistently showcases pride of ownership. Striking streetscapes, well-groomed grounds, and tidy common areas preserve your investment.

  • Collaborative architectural oversight to protect property values.
  • Vibrant common elements and continuous site care.
  • Active partnerships with property owners and services.

The Northland Advantage

📍 Exceptional KC Connectivity

Located off NE Shady Lane Dr in Kansas City, MO (Zip 64119), our residents enjoy unmatched regional convenience. Avoid long commutes while remaining tucked away safely from high-traffic expressways.

  • Quick 15-minute commute to downtown businesses.
  • Easy interstate access to Kansas City International Airport (MCI).
  • Minutes away from sprawling shopping options at Zona Rosa and Liberty.

🎓 Highly-Rated Public Education

Growing households benefit significantly from placement within the acclaimed North Kansas City School District (NKC Schools), renowned across Missouri for advanced learning, diverse programming, and high graduation rates.

  • State-recognized early childhood and career-readiness programs.
  • Robust options for secondary student athletics and artistic pursuits.
  • High community-backed investment in school facility modernization.

🌳 Local Parks & Entertainment

The Northland region features a bounty of outdoor trails, pristine public spaces, family-centric entertainment hubs, and famous culinary institutions, blending suburban space with urban culture.

  • Proximity to historic parks, public golf courses, and paved paths.
  • Short drive to Oceans of Fun and Worlds of Fun theme parks.
  • Endless dining options showcasing world-class Kansas City BBQ.
