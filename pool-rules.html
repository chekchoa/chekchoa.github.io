Carriage Hill Estates HOA

Private Community Pool Guidelines & Safety Regulations

Pool Schedule & Access

Early-Access Hours

6:30 AM – 8:00 AM
  • Open exclusively to registered CHE/HOA members over the age of 18.
  • Strictly reserved for lap swimming, water aerobics, and structured exercise.
  • No guests are permitted during this window to ensure uninterrupted workouts.

Maintenance Window

8:00 AM – 10:00 AM
  • Pool is closed to all members and guests.
  • Required maintenance activities and safety inspections are performed during this time.
  • Our pool contractor may perform servicing at any point during this window.

Normal Operating Hours

10:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Open to all CHE/HOA members and their accompanied guests.
  • Maximum of 6 guests per household; guests must be accompanied by at least one member.
  • Children and teens under the age of 16 must be supervised by a member over the age of 18.
  • Pool party table reservations are available, though the pool remains shared and accessible to all members.

Pool Rules & Guidelines

Safety First: The Carriage Hill Estates pool is a private community amenity. Please review the safety and facility rules below to ensure a fun, safe, and clean environment for all families.

Safety & Prohibitions

  • Absolutely No Glass permitted in or around the pool area.
  • No Diving allowed at any time.
  • No Running on the pool deck or surrounding areas.
  • No Smoking or Vaping anywhere within the pool facility.
  • Keep off the safety rope.
  • No Pets allowed inside the pool gates.

Conduct & Environment

  • No Unnecessary Roughness or unsafe play.
  • No Abusive or Foul Language; keep it family-friendly.
  • No Alcoholic Beverages permitted on the premises.
  • No External Music (please use personal headphones).
  • Don’t sit on the steps to keep entryways clear for others.
  • Large inflatables and large floats are not permitted.

Dress Code & Facility Care

  • Appropriate Swimwear Required: Family-friendly attire only. No thong or cheeky bottoms permitted.
  • Keep Food and Drink 10 feet away from the pool edge.
  • Pick Up Your Trash and help keep our community beautiful.
  • Clean Up Your Area before leaving.
  • Put Chairs Back in their proper place before you leave.
  • Put Umbrellas Down after use to protect them from wind damage.
