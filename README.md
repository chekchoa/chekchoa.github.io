# [Carriage Hill Estates](https://carriagehillestates.org)
---

Carriage Hill Estates is an established residential neighborhood located in the Northland area of Kansas City, Missouri, within Clay County. Our community is characterized by its tree-lined streets, well-tended lawns, and a quiet, welcoming atmosphere that offers residents a peaceful place to unwind.

Life in Carriage Hill Estates balances a relaxed neighborhood setting with everyday practicality. We are situated with convenient access to local shopping, dining, and community entertainment, and are served by highly-regarded local schools. It is a neighborhood where families and individuals alike can enjoy a supportive community environment and a comfortable place to call home.
